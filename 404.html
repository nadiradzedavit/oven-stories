Our story

A small kitchen with big flavor.

Oven Stories started as a homemade project in Tbilisi — a cloud kitchen quietly turning out the tiramisu everyone in the city started talking about. Today we bake everything fresh each morning: classic and flavored tiramisu, fragrant biryani, flaky puffs and cakes made to order. No shortcuts. Just good food, packed with care, brought to your door.
